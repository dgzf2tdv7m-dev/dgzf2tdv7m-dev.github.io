TradeInvoice One Privacy Policy

TradeInvoice One creates clients, estimates, invoices, PDFs, and CSV files locally on your device. It does not track users, show ads, sell personal information, or share company, client, or billing data with third parties.

Data storage

No account is required. Client details, company information, line items, payment notes, and invoice history are stored in the on-device SwiftData database.

Export

PDF and CSV files are generated only when you choose to export or share them, and iOS handles the selected share destination.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@fengkit.com. Version 1.0, updated June 21, 2026.
