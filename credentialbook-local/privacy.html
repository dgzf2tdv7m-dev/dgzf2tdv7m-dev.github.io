CredentialBook Local Privacy Policy

CredentialBook Local is a paid-download iOS app. It stores user-created records locally and does not use accounts, advertising, analytics SDKs, tracking, subscriptions, IAP, or free trials.

Core skeleton: 列表+详情. Business keywords: credential, certificate, expiry, renewal, archive, offline.

Data

Exports are created only when the user chooses a system share action. Deleting the app removes local app data unless the user exported a backup.
