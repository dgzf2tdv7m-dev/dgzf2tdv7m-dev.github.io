Common Questions

Does AngelVault US replace a will?
No. It is not a legal document replacement. Use a lawyer and formal estate documents for legal instructions.

Does the app send messages automatically?
The local MVP prepares messages in Apple system Mail and Messages. The user must confirm sending in the system interface.

Where is my data stored?
Your capsules are stored locally on your device and encrypted with a key in the local Keychain.

What if I delete the app?
Deleting the app may delete local encrypted content. Keep traditional backups and legal documents outside the app.
