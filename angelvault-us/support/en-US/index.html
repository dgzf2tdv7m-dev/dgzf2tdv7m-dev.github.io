AngelVault Support Page

Last updated: June 21, 2026

AngelVault is a calm digital legacy vault for the words, files, and instructions your trusted people may need if you cannot check in.

Create encrypted information capsules for family instructions, insurance notes, recovery hints, personal letters, photos, and files. Choose trusted recipients, set a check-in rhythm, and let AngelVault remind you to confirm you are okay.

AngelVault is not a will, trust, power of attorney, attorney, or legal document. Delivery depends on your device, network, Apple notification services, and system Mail or Messages, so delivery cannot be guaranteed.
