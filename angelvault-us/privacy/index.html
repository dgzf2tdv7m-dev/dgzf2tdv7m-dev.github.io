AngelVault US Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

AngelVault US is designed as a local encrypted vault. We do not collect, sell, or track personal data.

Data Stored On Device

Information capsules, recipients, notes, photos, and files are stored locally on your device. Capsule content and attachments are encrypted with CryptoKit AES-GCM. The encryption key is stored in the local Keychain.

No Account and No Tracking

AngelVault US does not require an account, does not use advertising identifiers, and does not include third-party analytics.

Mail and Messages

When you choose to release a capsule, AngelVault US prepares content in Apple system Mail or Messages compose interfaces. Sending is handled by the system app and requires user confirmation.

Data Loss

If you delete the app, erase your device, or lose access to the local Keychain, encrypted content may not be recoverable.
