PoolChem Plain Privacy Policy

PoolChem Plain is a paid utility that calculates pool volume, chlorine, pH, alkalinity dosing, PDF maintenance cards, and local care logs on the user's device. It does not require an account, does not collect personal information, does not use analytics, does not track users, and does not send project data or exports to a server.

Local Data

Projects, settings, history entries, imported files, generated PDF documents, and SVG exports remain under the user's control. The app stores small records locally only so users can resume their own work.

No Tracking

The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, login accounts, remote databases, or server-side processing services.
