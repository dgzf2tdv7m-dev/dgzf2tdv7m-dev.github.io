RPAS Deck CA Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

RPAS Deck CA is a paid offline study and practice tool for Canadian RPAS exam preparation. The app is designed to keep study activity on the user's device.

Data Collection

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not require an account and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

On-Device Data

Quiz results, missed questions, progress summaries, and study preferences are stored locally on the device so the app can show review history and progress. This data is not transmitted to us.

Network Access

The app's core features work without a network connection. It does not upload study content, quiz answers, or device identifiers to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users preparing for aviation knowledge exams and is not directed to children under 13.

Contact
