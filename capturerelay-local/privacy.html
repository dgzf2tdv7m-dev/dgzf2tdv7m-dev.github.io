CaptureRelay Local Privacy Policy

CaptureRelay Local captures text notes, voice files, selected photos, routes, queue records and receipts locally until the user sends or exports them. The app does not require an account, does not track users, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not send saved records to a server unless the user chooses an external route or share action.

Local Data

Records, settings, imported media, reminders, generated documents, and export receipts remain under the user's control. Users can delete local records from the app or remove the app from the device.

No Tracking

The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, login accounts, remote databases, or server-side processing services.
