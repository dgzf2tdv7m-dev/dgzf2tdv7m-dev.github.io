StudioRack CA Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

Overview

StudioRack CA is an offline iOS utility for studio gear inventory, patch notes, session preparation, maintenance logs, local reminders, and cost records.

Data Collection

StudioRack CA does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data. App content is intended to remain on the user's device and is not sent to our servers.

  • No account registration or sign-in.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling.
  • No developer-operated cloud synchronization.
  • No third-party SDKs for analytics, ads, attribution, or crash reporting.
  • No payment processing or external user database.

Local Storage and Notifications

Gear details, serial numbers, client fields, session notes, patch notes, maintenance notes, reminder settings, and cost entries are stored locally on the device using standard app storage. Optional local notifications are disabled until enabled by the user and are managed by iOS permissions.

User Control

Because the app does not operate an account service or remote database, we do not have access to view, export, correct, or delete user-created app content. Users control their local records in the app, through iOS settings, or by deleting the app and its local data from the device.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the StudioRack CA support page.
