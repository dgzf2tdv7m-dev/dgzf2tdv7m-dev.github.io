StudioRack CA Support

Help for StudioRack CA, an offline studio gear and session log for iOS.

Using the App

Use the Gear, Patch, Sessions, Costs, and Settings tabs to manage local studio records, patch recall notes, session preparation, maintenance reminders, and expense entries.

Privacy

StudioRack CA stores user-created records locally on the device. It does not require an account, use advertising, collect analytics, or sync data to a developer-operated cloud service.

Contact

For support questions, contact the developer through the App Store support channel for StudioRack CA.
