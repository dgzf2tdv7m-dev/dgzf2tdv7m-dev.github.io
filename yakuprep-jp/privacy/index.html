YakuPrep JP Privacy Policy

YakuPrep JP is an offline paid exam preparation app.

No personal data is collected, sold, tracked, or shared. The app does not use accounts, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, in-app purchases, or subscriptions.

Study progress, notes, bookmarks, wrong-answer history, and reports remain on the user's device unless the user exports or shares them.
