DuctSizer Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-20

DuctSizer is designed as a focused utility. We do not sell personal data, track users across apps, or build advertising profiles.

Data

App records, notes, calculations, photos selected by you, observing plans, lap sessions, or field logs remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Permissions

The app requests only permissions needed for its stated features, such as location, photo selection, or camera access depending on the product.

Payments

Lifetime unlocks are processed by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card information.
