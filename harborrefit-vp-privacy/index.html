HarborRefit VP Privacy & Support

Bundle ID: com.marinerefit.nz.vision. Last updated: 2026-06-23.

Privacy Policy

HarborRefit VP is a paid upfront, offline-first app for keeping structured local records. All features are unlocked after purchase. No account, ads, in-app purchases, tracking, or cloud sync are included.

HarborRefit VP does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit personal data. Records stay on the user's device.

No account, no login, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, no cloud sync, and no in-app purchases are included.

Support

For support, contact the developer through the App Store support channel. Include the app name, device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
