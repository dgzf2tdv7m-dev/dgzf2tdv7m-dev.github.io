EZCalc Diamonds Privacy Policy

EZCalc Diamonds is an offline paid iOS app. It does not collect, sell, transmit, track, or share personal data.

  • No account is required.
  • No analytics SDK, ad SDK, or third-party tracking SDK is included.
  • All app records are stored locally on the user's device through SwiftData.
  • Exports are created only when the user chooses to share a file.
