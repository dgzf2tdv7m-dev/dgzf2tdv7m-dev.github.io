TurfHouse IS Privacy Policy

Bundle ID: com.turfhouse.is. Last updated: 2026-06-17.

Data Collection

TurfHouse IS does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit personal data. Records entered in the app stay on the user's device.

Offline Use

The app is designed as an offline record keeping aid. It does not include account sign-in, cloud sync, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, third-party tracking, or in-app purchases.

Support

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the App Store support channel for this app.
