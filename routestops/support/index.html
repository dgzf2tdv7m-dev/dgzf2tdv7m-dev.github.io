RouteStops Pro Support

For help with RouteStops Pro, contact support@fengkit.com with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Basic workflow

Add individual addresses, paste a batch list, or pick an address from contacts. Tap Optimize to reorder stops, Copy to copy the stop manifest, or PDF to export the route list.

Data handling

Route data stays local on the device. Exports are created only when you choose an iOS share or save action, and deleting the app deletes local app data unless you have exported a copy.
