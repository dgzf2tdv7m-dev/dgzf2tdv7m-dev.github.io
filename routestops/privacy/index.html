RouteStops Pro Privacy Policy

RouteStops Pro is a local-first multi-stop route planning app. Route stops, addresses, sequence order, and route history are stored locally on your device.

Data handling

The app does not track users, does not use an account system, does not show ads, and does not sell or share personal data with third parties. Stop ordering and route planning calculations run locally.

Exports

PDF exports are created only when you choose an iOS share or save action. Deleting the app deletes local app data unless you have exported a copy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@fengkit.com. Version 1.0, updated June 21, 2026.
