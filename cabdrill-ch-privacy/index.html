Privacy Policy

SwissTaxiTest CH is a focused utility app designed for the CH market. Key features: - Clean, native SwiftUI interface - Works completely offline — no account required - All data stays private on your device - Optimized for iPhone, iOS 17+

CabDrill CH does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit personal data. Records stay on the user's device.

No account, no login, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, no cloud sync, and no in-app purchases are included.
