ExamCards CA Support

Support for the paid iOS app ExamCards CA.

Getting Help

Email support@dgzf2tdv7m.dev with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

Review Workflow

Create decks, add front/back cards, flip during review, and mark each card as mastered, review again, or new.

Reminders

Review reminders are local iOS notifications. They can be changed or disabled in the app or through iOS notification settings.

Response Time

Support requests are usually reviewed within three business days.
