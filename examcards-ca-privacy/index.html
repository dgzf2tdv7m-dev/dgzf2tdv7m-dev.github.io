ExamCards CA Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

ExamCards CA is a paid flashcard and spaced-review tool for Canadian study workflows.

Data Collection

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not require an account and does not include advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Study Data

Decks, cards, review sessions, mastery status, and reminder settings are stored locally on the device. This study content is not transmitted to us.

Notifications

If reminders are enabled, iOS may display local notifications scheduled by the app. Reminder data stays on the device.

Network Access

The app's core features work offline. It does not upload cards, review history, or device identifiers to external servers.
