SleepLab US Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-20

SleepLab US is an offline-first utility. We do not sell personal data, run third-party advertising tracking, or build cross-app profiles.

Local data

Files, recordings, database references, network scan history, and generated exports stay on your device unless you choose to share or export them.

Permissions

The app requests only permissions needed for its core function, such as microphone, local network, or document access. Purchases are processed by Apple StoreKit.
