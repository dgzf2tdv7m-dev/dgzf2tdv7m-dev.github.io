GitPocket FI Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-20

GitPocket FI is designed as an offline-first app. We do not sell personal data, track users across apps, or operate an advertising profile.

Data

Your projects, scans, practice progress, notes, records, or drafts remain on your device unless you explicitly export or share them.

Permissions

The app requests only the permissions needed for its features, such as camera, files, notifications, speech recognition, or photo export depending on the product.
