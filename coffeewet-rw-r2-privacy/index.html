Coffeewet RW R2 Privacy Policy

CoffeeWet RW R2 is a paid offline app. It does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

No tracking. The app includes no analytics SDK, advertising SDK, third-party tracking SDK, subscription, IAP, cloud sync, or payment flow.

All records stay on the user's device through local Apple system storage unless the user manually exports or shares information outside the app.
