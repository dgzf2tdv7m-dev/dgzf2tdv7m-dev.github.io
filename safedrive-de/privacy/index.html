SafeDrive DE Privacy Policy

Offline safety driving assistant with local warnings, logbook and monthly mileage statistics.

Data Collection

SafeDrive DE does not collect, sell, rent, or transmit personal data to our servers. The app is designed as a paid upfront local utility.

Local Storage

Project data, logs, notes, settings, images, exports, and calculations are stored locally on your device when you choose to create them.

Network and Accounts

The app does not require an account, subscription, cloud sync, online payment connection, or third-party analytics service.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, email support@fengkit.com.
