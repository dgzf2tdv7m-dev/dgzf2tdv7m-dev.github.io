GradeBox US Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-20

GradeBox US is designed as an offline-first utility. We do not sell personal data, run third-party advertising tracking, or build cross-app profiles.

Local data

Records you create remain on your device unless you choose to export, save, or share them. App-specific records may include labels, scans, training clips, class rosters, scores, attendance, schedules, or generated reports depending on the product.

Permissions

The app requests only permissions required for its core features, such as camera access for scanning or video recording. Payment unlocks are processed by Apple StoreKit.
