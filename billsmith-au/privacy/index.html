BillSmith AU Privacy Policy

Local quote and invoice builder for Australian small businesses with GST and PDF export.

Data Collection

BillSmith AU does not collect, sell, rent, or transmit personal data to our servers. The app is designed as a paid upfront local utility.

Local Storage

Project data, logs, notes, settings, images, exports, and calculations are stored locally on your device when you choose to create them.

Network and Accounts

The app does not require an account, subscription, cloud sync, online payment connection, or third-party analytics service.
