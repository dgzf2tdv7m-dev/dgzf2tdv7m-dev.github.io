TapForms MicroDB is a paid-download iOS app. It stores user-created records locally and does not use accounts, advertising, analytics SDKs, tracking, subscriptions, IAP, or free trials.

Core skeleton: 列表+表单. Business keywords: custom form, barcode, photo field, CSV import, iCloud backup, PDF checklist.
