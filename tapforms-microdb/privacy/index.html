TapForms MicroDB Privacy Policy

TapForms MicroDB stores user-created records on the device and writes export files only when the user chooses a share, save, or backup action.

Local Data

Projects, forms, site cards, work orders, inventory entries, histories, reminders, and generated PDF or CSV files remain under the user's control. The app does not require an account or remote database.

No Tracking

The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, login accounts, remote processing, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.
