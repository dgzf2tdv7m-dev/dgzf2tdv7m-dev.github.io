SawmillLedger CA Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

App Overview

SawmillLedger CA is a local record book for small Canadian sawmills, portable mill operators, farm woodlots, and woodworking yards that need an offline way to organize day-to-day milling records.

This policy applies to the iOS app identified by bundle ID com.sawmillledger.ca.

Data Collection

SawmillLedger CA does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data. App content is intended to remain on the user's device and is not sent to our servers.

  • No account registration or sign-in.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling.
  • No cloud synchronization operated by the app.
  • No third-party SDKs for analytics, ads, attribution, or crash reporting.
  • No payment processing or external user database.

Local Storage

Records, notes, preferences, and optional reminders created in SawmillLedger CA are stored locally using Apple's standard app storage. Removing the app may remove this local information. Optional local notification permissions are handled by iOS and can be changed in Settings.

User Control

Because the app does not operate an account service or remote database, we do not have access to view, export, correct, or delete user-created app content. Users control local data directly on their device.

Contact
