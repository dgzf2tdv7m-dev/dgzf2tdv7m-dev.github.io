DoseBell Family Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

DoseBell Family is a local-first medication reminder app. Medication names, doses, schedules, inventory, family board views, and dose history stay in the app's local SwiftData database on your device.

We do not track users, do not use an account system, do not show ads, and do not sell or share personal or health data with third parties.

The app may ask for notification permission to send medication and low-stock reminders. These reminders are local notifications generated on your device.

DoseBell Family does not integrate with HealthKit in this version. PDF exports leave the device only when you choose to share them through the iOS share sheet.
