DoseBell Family Support

Common Questions

How do I add a half tablet? Add a medication on the medication screen and use the 0.5 dose step for half tablets, 1.5 tablets, and similar schedules.

Where do reminders come from? The app uses iOS local notifications for daily dose reminders and low-inventory reminders.

Does the family board sync to the cloud? No. The family board is an on-device view for a shared device or in-person caregiver review.

How do I export history? Open History and choose the PDF export action to share a dose history report.
