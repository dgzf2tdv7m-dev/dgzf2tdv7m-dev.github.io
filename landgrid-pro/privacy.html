LandGrid Pro Privacy Policy

LandGrid Pro is a paid iOS field utility for recording LSD/NTS coordinates, parcel folders, field photos, timestamps, and local export files for Canadian land work.

Data is stored locally on the device with SwiftData. The app does not use accounts, advertising, analytics SDKs, tracking, or remote sync.

Files and photos

GeoJSON, KML, CSV, and photo actions happen only after the user opens the system picker or chooses an export destination.
