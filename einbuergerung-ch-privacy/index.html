Einbürgerung CH Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-26

Einbürgerung CH is designed as an exam preparation and study utility. We do not sell personal information.

Information We Collect

The app stores study progress and settings locally on your device. We do not require account registration and we do not collect names, email addresses, precise location, contacts, photos, or payment card information.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use third-party advertising trackers. If Apple provides crash diagnostics or basic App Store analytics, that information is handled under Apple's policies and is used only to improve app quality.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with advertisers or data brokers. Local study data remains on your device unless you choose to back up your device using Apple services.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@example.com
