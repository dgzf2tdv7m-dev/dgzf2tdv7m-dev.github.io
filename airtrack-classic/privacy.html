AirTrack Classic Privacy Policy

AirTrack Classic stores user-created settings, logs, recipes, layouts, reports, thresholds, and export files on the device. The app does not require an account, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking permission, subscription, or server database.

Local Data

Device lists, ingredient libraries, shift logs, border reports, PDF files, JPEG cards, iCal exports, and saved preferences remain under the user's control on the device unless the user shares a generated file.

No Tracking

The app does not include cross-app tracking, third-party analytics, advertising identifiers, remote accounts, or in-app purchase code.
