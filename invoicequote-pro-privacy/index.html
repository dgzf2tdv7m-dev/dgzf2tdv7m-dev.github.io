InvoiceQuote Pro Privacy Policy

InvoiceQuote Pro is an offline document app for creating invoices, quotes, receipts, and orders.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data. Documents, clients, and item templates are stored locally on your device.

Accounts, Ads, and Tracking

The app has no account system, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.

PDF Sharing

When you choose to share or print a PDF, iOS presents the system share sheet. The destination you select controls how that exported file is handled.

Contact

For support, contact the developer through the App Store support link.
