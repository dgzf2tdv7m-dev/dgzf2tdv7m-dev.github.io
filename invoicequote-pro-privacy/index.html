InvoiceQuote Pro Privacy Policy

InvoiceQuote Pro is an offline document tool for quotes, invoices, receipts, and orders. The app does not require an account and does not send document, client, item, or PDF data to our servers.

Data Storage

All business records are stored locally on your device using Apple system storage. You control exported PDFs and any files you choose to share.

No Tracking

The app contains no ads, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.
