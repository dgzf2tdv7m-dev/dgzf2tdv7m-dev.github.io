TelePrompt CA Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

TelePrompt CA is a paid script editor and teleprompter for recording, speaking, and rehearsal workflows.

Data Collection

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not require an account and does not include advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Script Storage

Scripts, titles, playback speed, font size, and mirror-mode settings are stored locally on the device using iOS storage. Script content is not transmitted to us.

Camera and Microphone

The app is a text teleprompter and does not need camera or microphone access for its core features.

Network Access

The app's core features work offline. It does not upload scripts, playback settings, or device identifiers to external servers.
