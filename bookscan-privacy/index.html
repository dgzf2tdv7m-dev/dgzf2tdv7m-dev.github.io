BookScan US Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-20

BookScan US is designed as an offline-first utility. We do not sell personal data, run third-party advertising tracking, or build cross-app profiles.

Local data

Records you create remain on your device unless you choose to export, save, or share them. Depending on the app, local records can include site photos, punch items, connection targets, classroom books, or loan records.

Network and permissions

Camera access is used only for capture or barcode scanning. Local network access is used only by AirMouse to find and connect to receivers you choose. Apple processes lifetime purchases through StoreKit.
