FloraID US Privacy & Support

Bundle ID: com.codexpipeline.floraid. Last updated: 2026-06-23.

Privacy Policy

Collect plant photos, browse an offline encyclopedia, and keep watering, feeding, and repotting on schedule. - Save plant photos with discovery date, place notes, and collection category. - Browse a bundled encyclopedia with common names, families, origins, and care notes. - Plan watering, feeding, and repotting tasks in a simple care calendar. - Filter and favorite indoor, outdoor, succulent, flowering, and herb entries. - Unlock unlimited plants, offline encyclopedia access, and full care scheduling.

FloraID US does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit personal data. Records stay on the user's device.

No account, no login, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, no cloud sync, and no in-app purchases are included.

Support

For support, contact the developer through the App Store support channel. Include the app name, device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
