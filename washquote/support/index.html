WashQuote Pro Support

Getting Started

Open the New Quote tab, choose a service preset, enter square footage and costs, then save the quote to a customer or export a PDF proposal.

Customer History

The Customers tab stores names, addresses, phone numbers, and quote history locally on the device.

Company Settings

Use Settings to edit company information, default rates, truck roll fee, tax rate, currency, and quote validity.

Contact

Email support@washquotepro.app with the device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
