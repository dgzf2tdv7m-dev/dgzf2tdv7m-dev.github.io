WashQuote Pro Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Data Collection

WashQuote Pro does not collect personal data, track users, show ads, use an account system, or transmit customer information to external servers.

Local Calculations

Pressure washing quote totals, service presets, taxes, and fees are calculated locally on your device.

Local Storage

Company settings, customer records, and quote history are stored locally on the device using UserDefaults. PDF files are generated only when you choose to export or share a quote.

Third Parties

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, third-party sharing, or cloud sync services.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact support@fengkit.com.
