WashQuote Pro Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Data Collection

WashQuote Pro does not collect personal data, track users, show ads, or transmit customer information to external servers.

Local Storage

Company settings, customer records, and quote history are stored locally on the device using UserDefaults. PDF files are generated only when the user chooses to export or share a quote.

Third Parties

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, account systems, or cloud sync services.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact support@washquotepro.app.
