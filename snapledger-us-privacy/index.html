Privacy Policy

SnapLedger is a receipt scanning and personal expense tracking utility. This policy explains how the app handles information on your device.

Receipt Data

SnapLedger may process receipt images and extracted text that you choose to scan. Receipt records are intended for personal organization and review.

Local Storage

Scanned receipt details and app preferences may be stored locally on your device. SnapLedger does not sell personal information and does not include third-party advertising trackers.

Financial Disclaimer

SnapLedger is for personal expense tracking only. It does not replace professional accounting, bookkeeping, tax advice, or official tax filing records.
