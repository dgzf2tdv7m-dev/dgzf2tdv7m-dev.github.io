MyGarage Log Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

MyGarage Log is a local vehicle maintenance log. We do not sell, rent, or share your vehicle profiles, maintenance records, reminders, or cost history.

Local Storage

Vehicles, service records, reminders, and cost statistics are stored in the on-device SwiftData database. No account is required.

Tracking and Ads

The app does not track users, show ads, use third-party analytics SDKs, or share data with third parties.

Notifications

The app may request system notification permission for maintenance due reminders. You can turn notifications off at any time in iOS Settings.

Exports

CSV and PDF files are generated only when you choose to export them, and iOS handles the selected share or save destination.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@fengkit.com.
