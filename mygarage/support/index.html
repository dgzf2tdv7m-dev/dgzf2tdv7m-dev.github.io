MyGarage Log Support

Use MyGarage Log to manage vehicles, record maintenance, set reminders, and export reports from one local workspace.

Basic Workflow

  • Add a vehicle from the Vehicles screen with make, model, year, VIN, plate, and current mileage.
  • Record service from the Records screen with date, mileage, service items, cost, shop, and notes.
  • Set reminders from the Reminders screen by date, mileage, or both.
  • Export CSV or PDF reports from the Stats screen, then send or save them with the system share sheet.

Support Scope

The app is a local tool and does not require an account. For data or export issues, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce the issue.
