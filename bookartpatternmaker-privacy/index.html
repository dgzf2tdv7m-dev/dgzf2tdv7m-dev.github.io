BookArt Pattern Maker Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

No tracking

BookArt Pattern Maker does not track users, does not show ads, and does not include third-party analytics SDKs.

Local data

Projects, calculations, pins, histories, and exports are stored locally on the user's device unless the user manually shares an exported file.

Accounts and purchases

The app is a paid download. It has no in-app purchases, subscriptions, free trials, or account system.
