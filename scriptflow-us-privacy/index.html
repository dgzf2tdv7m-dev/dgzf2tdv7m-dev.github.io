ScriptFlow US Privacy & Support

Bundle ID: com.codexpipeline.scriptflow. Last updated: 2026-06-23.

Privacy Policy

ScriptFlow is built for people who need a private, offline-first workspace on iPhone and iPad. - Import or paste plain text and Markdown scripts. - Run a full-screen prompter with speed, size, and mirror controls. - Use speech-paced pause and resume for more natural takes. - Keep creator scripts grouped by show, course, or campaign. - Record with a clean monitor-style overlay. - ScriptFlow is a production aid, not a safety broadcast system.

ScriptFlow US does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit personal data. Records stay on the user's device.

No account, no login, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, no cloud sync, and no in-app purchases are included.

Support

For support, contact the developer through the App Store support channel. Include the app name, device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
