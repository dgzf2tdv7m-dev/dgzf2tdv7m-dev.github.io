Privacy Policy

Time track-day laps, compare sectors, and read the numbers clearly from a high-contrast cockpit display. - Use GPS-assisted start and finish detection for closed-course lap timing. - Set track sectors manually and compare split deltas in real time. - Read current lap, best lap, and sector gap from a large high-contrast display. - Review lap history with simple charts and export-ready session summaries. - Unlock unlimited tracks, CSV export, and detailed sector analysis.

LapTrack US does not collect, sell, share, track, or transmit personal data. Records stay on the user's device.

No account, no login, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, no cloud sync, and no in-app purchases are included.

Support

For support, contact the developer through the App Store support channel. Include the app name, device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
